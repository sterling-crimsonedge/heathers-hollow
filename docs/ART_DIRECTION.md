# Art Direction — Heather's Hollow

> **Status:** TODO — placeholder. Build out as references are gathered.

## North star

Cottagecore kawaii. A village that feels handcrafted, warm, and a little bit storybook.

## Inspirations

- **Animal Crossing** — silhouette language, scale, friendliness
- **Neko Atsume** — flat-but-charming forms, restraint
- **Bamboletta dolls** — handmade textile warmth, soft faces
- **Porcelain dishware** — floral motifs, glaze, ceramic shine

## Form language

- Rounded everything. No sharp corners.
- Slightly oversized heads on characters.
- Buildings that lean slightly — handmade, not architectural.

## Texture

- Visible weave on fabrics
- Soft brushwork on painted surfaces
- Gentle subsurface scattering on skin/petals
- Avoid: chrome, plastic, sci-fi sheen

## Lighting

- Warm key, cool fill
- Long golden-hour shadows by default
- Soft volumetric haze in the morning

## Shaders (to build)

- Toon/ramp shader with painterly edges
- Cloth shader with weave detail
- Foliage shader that responds to wind & footsteps
